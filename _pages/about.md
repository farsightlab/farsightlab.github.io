We build AI systems that understand video, anticipate what comes next, and plan over long horizons — for first-person assistants, multi-agent strategic settings, and physical AI. Our work is driven by a central idea: that **strategic reasoning over dynamic scenes is the next frontier beyond perception**.

The lab is led by [Lorenzo Torresani](/about/) at Northeastern University's [Khoury College of Computer Sciences](https://www.khoury.northeastern.edu/) and is a founding group of the [Physical AI Research (PAIR) Center](https://www.northeastern.edu/).

We are recruiting **postdocs, visiting researchers, and PhD students** to work on embodied AI, video understanding, and multimodal learning. Prospective applicants should contact Lorenzo with a CV and a one-page research statement.
